Animal Profiles

cow

Cows are gentle farm animals that provide milk and help humans in agriculture. They are social and love to stay in herds.

bee

Bees collect nectar to make honey and are essential pollinators that help plants grow and produce fruit.

octopus

Octopuses are intelligent sea creatures with eight arms and incredible camouflage skills. They can change color to hide from danger.

zebra

Zebras are wild African animals known for their black and white stripes, which help them blend into grassy habitats.

giraffe

Giraffes are the tallest land animals, using their long necks to eat leaves from tall trees in African savannas.

snail

Snails move slowly using a single foot and carry a spiral shell for protection. They love moist, shady places.

otter

Otters are playful swimmers that use tools to crack open shells. They hold hands while resting to stay together.

Blue Whale

The blue whale is the largest animal on Earth, feeding mainly on tiny krill. Its heart can weigh as much as a car.

penguin

Penguins are flightless birds that live in cold regions. They waddle on land but swim gracefully underwater.

Seal

Seals are playful marine mammals that spend time both in the ocean and on land. They’re great swimmers and divers.
