Animal fun fact about ORGANS

The Tongue Titans

As the largest animals on Earth, blue whales are truly gigantic.
Just a blue whale’s tongue can weigh more than an entire elephant — that’s over 7,000 kilograms!

The Krill Filter Specialists

Crabeater Seals are the most numerous large animal on the planet, and they primarily eat tiny krill (not crabs!).
They have highly specialized cheek teeth that interlock like a fine sieve. When the seal gulps a mouthful of krill and water, the teeth lock together to strain the water out, trapping the krill inside for them to swallow.
This unique dental structure makes them the Antarctic's ultimate filter feeders.

The Pebble Proposers

Male gentoo and Adélie penguins “propose” to females by offering them a pebble. These pebbles are precious because penguins use them to build their nests, and they can be hard to find along the icy Antarctic shorelines.
If the female accepts the pebble, the pair bonds and mates for life.
